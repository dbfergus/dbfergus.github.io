What is SPARK?
SPARK is an innovative registered apprenticeship program designed to strengthen the early childhood education workforce in Forsyth County. We create debt-free pathways to ECE credentials while building a qualified, stable workforce for local childcare centers.
üë•
Who do we serve?
We serve high school students and young adults (18+) interested in early childhood education careers, and childcare centers seeking qualified, committed teaching staff. Our apprentices earn while they learn through structured workplace training and formal education.
üìç
Where does SPARK operate?
SPARK operates throughout Forsyth County, North Carolina, with a focus on Place Matters communities. Partner childcare centers provide workplace learning opportunities while apprentices attend classes at Forsyth Tech Community College.
üìÖ
When does the program start?
Our first cohort launches January 2026 with 10 apprentices. The program runs for 2 years, combining 4,000 hours of workplace learning with 848 hours of related education leading to an Associate degree and CDA credential.
